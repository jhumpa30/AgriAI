🌱 AgriAI

AgriAI is an AI-powered agricultural decision support system that helps farmers and agricultural stakeholders make informed decisions using machine learning.

Features
🔍 Crop Disease Detection
Upload a leaf image
Detects diseases across:
Rice
Maize
Potato
Tea
Tomato
Provides disease prediction and confidence score
❤️ Crop Health Assessment
Generates a crop health score based on disease detection results
⚠️ Disease Risk Prediction
Predicts disease risk using:
Temperature
Humidity
Rainfall
Health score
🌾 Yield Prediction
Predicts crop yield using:
Area
Rainfall
Fertilizer usage
Pesticide usage
Temperature data
💰 Market Price Prediction
Estimates market price using:
Demand index
Supply index
Inflation rate
Transport cost
Predicted yield
📋 Smart Recommendations
Generates disease-specific management recommendations
Provides health and risk-based advisory suggestions
